Welcome

How can I help your organization?


My name is Jonas Buchler. I'm a full-time husband, father of one (chihuahua), proud veteran, and career Intelligence Analyst turned Program Manager with a passion for software development and cloud solutions.


After 6 years as an Arabic Linguist in the U.S. Air Force leading and managing multiple NSA missions, I sought to expand my skillset in pursuit of my passion for technology. Seeking my next challenge and opportunity to learn, I was selected for the Microsoft Software and Systems Academy, where I learned the fundamental skills necessary to focus that passion towards Cloud Application Development.


As a career linguist and experienced manager in the Intelligence Community, I know how difficult it can be to translate customer and executive-directed objectives, deadlines, and restraints to the teams tasked with developing timely and accurate state-of-the-art products.


I've made a career of doing just that, by putting people first and employing Agile and Scrum methodologies. Modern problems require modern solutions; let me help your team get there.
